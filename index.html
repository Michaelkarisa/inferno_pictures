The Person Behind the Lens

Yuston Mwakesi

"Every story deserves to burn bright — with intention, raw emotion, and a frame that never lies."

Based in Nairobi and operating across Kenya and beyond, Yuston Mwakesi has spent seven years building Inferno Pictures into one of the most distinctive creative studios in East Africa. His work spans weddings, documentary film, real estate, and commercial photography.

His approach is instinctive, warm, and deeply intentional. Whether in a studio or chasing the last light on the Rift Valley, the result is always the same — imagery that stops time.
